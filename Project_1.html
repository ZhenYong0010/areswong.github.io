Project 1: IOT remote car

Introduction

This is my first IOT project that I build using ArduinoIDE and controlled by Blynk app in my mobile phone. The car can move forward, backward, left and right by controlling the button in the app. The car also equip with ultrasonic sensor to avoid obstacle in front of it. The car will stop moving when there is an obstacle detected within 10 cm.

How it works

The car is controlled by an Arduino UNO R3 board which is connected to an ESP8266 WiFi module. The Blynk app sends commands to the ESP8266 module via the internet, which then relays the commands to the Arduino board. The Arduino board controls the motors of the car using an L298N motor driver module. The ultrasonic sensor is used to detect obstacles in front of the car and stop it from moving if an obstacle is detected within 10 cm.

Components used

  • 1 unit LCD
  • 1 unit Arduino Wi Fi Module D1 R1
  • 2 units L298N controller
  • 1 unit humidity sensor
  • 1 unit distance sensor
  • 4 units DC motor
  • 4 units wheels
  • Power supply
  • 1 units breadboard
  • Several jumpers wire.
  • 2 units LED
  • 1 units Chassis
